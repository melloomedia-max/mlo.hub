Setup Notes
  1. Add RESEND_API_KEY on Railway under Variables.
  2. Verify melloo.media on Resend (DNS: DKIM TXT, SPF TXT, MX for bounces).
  3. Optional: set RESEND_WEBHOOK_SECRET + add https://hub.melloo.media/api/email/webhook/resend as a webhook in Resend.
  4. Set MAIL_FORCE_FALLBACK=1 to revert to Gmail in an incident.

⚠️ Danger Zone

Destructive actions that cannot be undone. Proceed with extreme caution.

⚠️ Purge Archived Data
Permanently delete all archived records from the local database archive tables. Drive copies remain.
⚠️ Reset All Portal Tokens
Invalidate all existing client portal links and generate new ones. Clients will need new links to access their portal.
⚠️ Clear Campaign History
Delete all campaign send records, enrollments, and analytics data. Campaign templates are preserved.
⚠️ Factory Reset
Delete ALL data and reset the application to its initial state. This is irreversible. All clients, invoices, tasks, campaigns, and settings will be permanently destroyed.
